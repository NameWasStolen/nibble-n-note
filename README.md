# Nibble n Notes

Ever had a craving for Carbonara but couldn't remember the restaurant you tried that made it best? 
Or what dishes you've tried in that Thai joint all that time ago? 

**Nibble n Notes** is a personal restaurant and food logging app that lets you:  
- Record restaurants you visit  
- Rate and review meals  
- Take photos and attach them for your personal records
- Log and comment on individual dishes served at restaurants

This is a project designed to showcase an android application with a focus on collating your personal food experiences with intuitive and user-friendly logging.

---

## Features

- **Restaurant Logging:** Keep track of restaurants you've visited with location and visit details.  
- **Meal and Dish Reviews:** Rate and write detailed reviews for meals and individual dishes.  
- **Photo Support:** Attach photos of dishes or restaurant experiences.

- **User Comments:** Add notes or comments on dishes and meals.
